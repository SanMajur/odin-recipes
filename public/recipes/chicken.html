Ingredient Notes

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Hot Sauce
  • Red Pepper Flakes

How To Make Firecracker Chicken

  1. Preheat your oven to 450 F degrees.
  2. Mix all the yummy sauce ingredients together.
  3. Place the chicken breasts with the bottom side up in the baking dish.
  4. Pour half the sauce over and use a brush to brush it evenly over the breasts.
  5. Flip the breasts over and pour the remaining sauce over them making sure it’s fully covered
  6. Transfer the baking dish, uncovered, to the preheated oven and bake for 18 to 25 minutes.
