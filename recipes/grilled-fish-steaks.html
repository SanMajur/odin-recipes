Grilled Fish Steaks

Description

These grilled fish steaks are marinated in a zesty mixture of lemon juice, garlic, and herbs, then cooked to perfection on the grill. They are flaky, tender, and full of flavor, making them a perfect main dish for any seafood lover.

Ingredients

  • 4 fish steaks (such as salmon or swordfish)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place the fish steaks in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them. Let marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Remove the fish from the marinade and grill for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  5. Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!
