Slow Cooker Lime Cilantro Chicken

Description

This slow cooker cilantro-lime chicken is packed with vibrant, zesty flavors. The tender, juicy chicken pairs perfectly with fresh lime and aromatic cilantro, making it a refreshing twist on plain chicken. Serve it over rice, toss it into salads, or use it as a filling for tacos and wraps for a deliciously versatile meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup lime juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the lime juice, cilantro, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the mixture over the chicken, making sure it's well coated.
  4. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  5. Shred the chicken with two forks and mix it with the juices in the slow cooker.
  6. Serve over rice, in tacos, or in salads.
