Ingredients

  • salmon
  • green onions
  • Garlic
  • ginger
  • olive oil
  • soy sauce
  • brown sugar
  • red pepper flakes
  • sriracha
  • black pepper

How To Make Firecracker Salmon

  1. Make the firecracker sauce by combining all the ingredients together
  2. Marinate the salmon.
    1. Put the salmon fillet in a large baking dish, skin side down if it has skin
    2. pour the marinade over the fish. Then rub the marinade all over the salmon making sure to fully cover both sides of the fish.
    3. Now, cover the baking dish with plastic wrap and put it in the fridge to marinate for at least 2 hours and up to 24 hours.
  3. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  4. Place the salmon into the preheated oven and bake for 15 minutes
  5. Garnish and serve - garnish it with fresh sliced green onions and serve imediately
